_(Specify this later) cantrip_

**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

You create a magic lantern that can float in the air and be controlled to move within 5 feet of you. The Lantern will light up everything within a 30 foot radius of the lamp.

**_At Higher Levels._** This spell creates a larger lantern when cast at higher levels. It will be able to move 5 feet further from your per level and shine 15 feet further. This effect maxes out at 5th level spells and will not go further.
