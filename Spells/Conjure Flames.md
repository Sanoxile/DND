_(Specify this later) cantrip_

**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You create a small flame that rests in the palm of your hand. You can use this as a light to light a the 15-foot radius around the flame. You can use an action to either light something on fire, or launch it up to 30ft away at a target dealing 1d8 fire damage. Both of these options will extinguish the flame.

**_At Higher Levels._** The spell’s gets another flame for every level you cast it up up to a max of 5 flames each dealing 1d8 fire damage and being able to light separate objects ablaze. This also increases the lighting radius by 15-feet for each flame.
